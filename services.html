Our Land Surveying Services

Comprehensive surveying and consulting services delivered with precision, advanced technology, and decades of expertise.

ALTA/NSPS Land Title Surveys

An ALTA/NSPS survey is required for most commercial real estate transactions. Our professionals coordinate accurate and compliant surveys that show all easements, right-of-ways, and any other elements that will impact a property. Turnaround times are crucial for these types of surveys, and we deliver on time.

  • Compliant with ALTA/NSPS Minimum Standard Detail Requirements
  • Complete easement and right-of-way identification
  • Zoning classification and setback requirements
  • Flood zone determination
  • Utility location and mapping
  • Title commitment review and exception plotting

Boundary Surveys

A boundary survey defines the limits of a property formally, focusing on the corners of a parcel of land. Whether you are buying, splitting, improving, or building on land, a boundary survey ensures you know exactly where your property lines are.

  • Property corner identification and monumentation
  • Deed research and analysis
  • Encroachment identification
  • Legal descriptions and plat preparation
  • Residential and commercial properties
  • Dispute resolution support
Learn More Request Boundary Survey Quote

Topographic Surveys

A topographic survey gathers information about the elevation of points on a piece of land and presents them as contour lines. Through the use of LiDAR, photogrammetry, and other advanced tools, we perform these surveys at significantly lower cost than traditional methods while delivering superior accuracy.

  • Elevation and contour mapping
  • LiDAR and photogrammetry data collection
  • Existing features and improvements location
  • Tree and vegetation surveys
  • Drainage and watershed analysis
  • Site development planning support
Learn More Request Topographic Survey Quote

Drone/UAV LiDAR & Photogrammetry Surveys

Our survey drones generate high-resolution orthomosaics and detailed 3D models of areas where low-quality, outdated, or no data is available. We enable high-accuracy cadastral maps to be produced quickly and easily, even in complex or difficult-to-access environments.

  • High-resolution orthomosaic imagery
  • 3D point cloud and terrain models
  • Volumetric calculations for stockpiles
  • Progress monitoring for construction sites
  • Difficult terrain and remote area access
  • FAA Part 107 certified pilots

Construction Layout & Staking

  • Grade and slope staking
  • As-built surveys
  • Subdivision plat preparation
  • Right-of-way surveys
