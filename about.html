Our Story

Built on Experience, Driven by Precision

The Land Consultants, LLC is a full-service land consulting firm providing land surveying, planning, management, and construction management services across Tennessee, Georgia, Alabama, Mississippi, and Kentucky.

With roots going back to 1996, our firm has grown into one of the region’s most trusted names in land surveying. We specialize in LiDAR, photogrammetry, and wireless telecom surveys, as well as land consulting and due diligence reports.

What sets us apart is the depth of expertise our principals bring to every project. We are not only Professional Land Surveyors — we also hold licenses as Real Estate Brokers, Contractors, and Commercial Pilots. This unique combination of credentials means we understand your land from every angle.
