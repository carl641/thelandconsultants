Huntsville, Alabama

Surveying the Rocket City’s Growth

Huntsville is one of the fastest-growing cities in the Southeast, driven by its thriving aerospace, defense, and technology sectors. This rapid growth creates enormous demand for professional land surveying — from new residential subdivisions on Monte Sano and Hampton Cove to commercial and industrial developments near Redstone Arsenal and Cummings Research Park.

The Land Consultants is licensed in Alabama and brings decades of expertise to Huntsville’s unique surveying challenges, including the area’s distinctive karst topography, varied terrain from river bottoms to mountain ridges, and the exacting requirements of defense and aerospace facility surveys.
