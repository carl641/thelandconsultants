Jasper, Tennessee • Main Office

Our Home Base in the Sequatchie Valley

Jasper, TN is home to The Land Consultants’ main office, located at 5449 US-41. From here, we serve clients throughout Marion County and the entire Sequatchie Valley region, as well as across Tennessee, Georgia, Alabama, Mississippi, and Kentucky.

Our strategic location provides easy access to projects across the tri-state area. The Sequatchie Valley’s mix of rural farmland, timber tracts, and growing residential developments creates diverse surveying needs — from large boundary surveys on mountain properties to subdivision plats for new residential communities.

Services in Jasper

Land Surveying Services We Offer

Complete surveying and consulting services for residential, commercial, and agricultural properties in Marion County.

ALTA/NSPS Surveys

Compliant title surveys for commercial transactions throughout the Sequatchie Valley and Marion County business corridor.

Boundary Surveys

Property boundary definition for the diverse terrain of Marion County, from valley floor parcels to mountain ridge properties.

Topographic Surveys

Detailed elevation and contour mapping essential for the valley’s varied topography, using LiDAR and photogrammetry for efficiency.

Drone/UAV LiDAR

Aerial mapping for the Sequatchie Valley’s large timber tracts, mountain properties, and difficult-to-access terrain.

Subdivision Surveys

Plat preparation and subdivision layout for Jasper’s growing residential development market.

Timber & Land Management

Timber valuation, volume surveys, and land management consulting for Marion County’s significant forest resources.
