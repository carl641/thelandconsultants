Ringgold, Georgia

Professional Land Surveying in Northwest Georgia

Ringgold and Catoosa County sit at the crossroads of Georgia’s Northwest corridor, experiencing steady residential and commercial growth driven by proximity to Chattanooga. The Land Consultants is licensed in Georgia and provides professional surveying services throughout the region.

From new residential developments along the I-75 corridor to agricultural properties in the surrounding countryside, our team understands the unique surveying requirements of Georgia properties, including compliance with Georgia’s Minimum Standards for Property Surveys.
