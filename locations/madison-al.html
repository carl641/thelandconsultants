Madison, Alabama

Surveying Madison’s Rapid Expansion

Madison, Alabama is consistently ranked among the fastest-growing and most desirable cities in the state. Its proximity to Huntsville’s aerospace industry, excellent school system, and family-friendly communities drive continuous residential and commercial development that requires professional land surveying.

The Land Consultants serves the Madison area with comprehensive surveying services, from boundary surveys for new homeowners to ALTA surveys for commercial developments along the US-72 corridor and County Line Road. Our team understands the specific requirements of Madison’s development codes and Alabama’s surveying standards.
