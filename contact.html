Survey costs vary depending on the type of survey, property size, terrain, and complexity. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your specific project needs.

We are licensed and provide surveying services in Tennessee, Georgia, Alabama, Mississippi, Kentucky, North Carolina, and Florida. Our team regularly serves Chattanooga, Jasper, Ringgold, Huntsville, Madison, and surrounding areas.

Timelines depend on the survey type and property complexity. Simple boundary surveys may be completed in a few days, while ALTA surveys for commercial properties may require more time for research and coordination. We always work to accommodate your timeline.

In most cases, you do not need to be present during the fieldwork. However, access to the property must be arranged. We’ll coordinate all logistics with you before beginning work.

Drone surveys provide high-resolution data for areas that are difficult to access by traditional means. They produce accurate 3D models and orthomosaics quickly and cost-effectively, making them ideal for large tracts, rugged terrain, and progress monitoring.
