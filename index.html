How It Works

Our Proven Process

From initial consultation to final deliverables, we make land surveying straightforward and stress-free.

1

Consultation

We discuss your project needs, review existing documents, and develop the ideal surveying approach for your property.

2

Research

Our team performs thorough deed research, reviews plats and records, and prepares for accurate fieldwork.

3

Field Survey

Using state-of-the-art equipment including UAV LiDAR and GPS, we collect precise data at your property.

4

Deliverables

You receive detailed drawings, plats, and reports to your specifications, ready for your project needs.
